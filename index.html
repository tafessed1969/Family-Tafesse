About Ethiopia

Ethiopia is one of the oldest nations in the world with thousands of years of history, cultural diversity, and unique traditions.

History of Ethiopia

Ethiopia is known for its ancient civilization, including the Aksumite Empire, one of the great civilizations of the ancient world. It is also one of the only African countries that was never colonized.

Geography

Ethiopia features highlands, lowlands, mountains, lakes, and the Great Rift Valley. It is the source of the Blue Nile River.

Economy

The economy is based on agriculture, services, and emerging industries. Major exports include coffee, gold, livestock, seeds, and horticulture.
